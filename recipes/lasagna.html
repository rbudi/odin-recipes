Lasagna Recipies

Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn't have be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

Lasagna Ingredients

These are the ingredient you'll need to make this lasagna recipe:

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sause
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodle
  • Water 1/2 cup

How to Make Lasagna

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minuts.
